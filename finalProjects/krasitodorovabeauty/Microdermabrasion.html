Microdermabrasion

Microdermabrasion Microdermabrasion is a non-invasive method, in which the surface layer of skin cells is removed with the aid of a special apparatus. In this way the renewal of the skin is stimulated physiologically, the surface is evened out and imperfect hydration is treated.

Microdermabrasion improves the circulation of blood, which leads to the stimulation of rejuvenation processes in the skin. The activity of the fibroblasts is increased and collagen and elastin fibers are formed, i.е. the tone and structure of the skin is improved. It becomes radiant; the pores shrink and fine wrinkles are erased.

The result is visible after just the first procedure. Depending on the problem being treated a series of procedures will be necessary, according to the type of skin and the gravity of the problem. Microdermabrasion is an elegant procedure; patients need no recovery period. The procedure can be performed all year round.
